## Title (working)

Attractor dynamics as a language substrate: limit cycles, proto-attractors, and multi-timescale control.

## Abstract

We describe a trainable continuous-state language model where token-conditioned dynamics follow explicit Euler integration with diffusion, centered cubic nonlinearities, and negative-distance logits to learned proto-attractors. Stabilization uses per-step coordinate clipping and dynamic norm renormalization. We outline hierarchical extensions and constraint-graph steering for future TS-OS integration.
