Once upon a time there was a small village by the sea.
The children ran through the market and laughed in the rain.
A quiet baker woke before dawn to warm the ovens.
At night, lanterns glowed and stories drifted through open windows.
People helped each other, shared bread, and sang old songs.
In winter, the wind was sharp, but the homes were warm.
In spring, flowers returned to the hills above the town.
A traveler arrived with maps, riddles, and a patient smile.
Each day brought work, kindness, and a little wonder.
And every evening ended with one more tale by candlelight.
